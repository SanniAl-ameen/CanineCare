Doberman

The Friendly and Intelligent Family Dog

About the Breed

Dobermans are sleek and powerful breed, known for their intelligence, loyalty and athleticism.

Key Facts

  • Origin: Germany
  • Size: Medium to Large Size, typically 24 - 28 inches tall
  • Weight: 60 - 80 pounds
  • Life Span: 10-13 years
  • Color: Black, Blue, red, Fawn

Temperament

Dobermans are known for their:

  • Athletic and agile
  • High intelligence and trainability
  • Confident and courageous
  • Strong desire to please their owners

Care Requirements

To keep a Doberman happy and healthy:

  • Daily exercise (such as daily walks and play time)
  • Regular grooming (brush 2-3 times weekly)
  • High-quality diet appropriate for large breeds
  • Early socialization and obedience training
  • Regular veterinary check-ups
