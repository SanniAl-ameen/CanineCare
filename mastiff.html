Mastiff

The Friendly and Intelligent Family Dog

About the Breed

Mastiffs Dogs are a giant breed known for their massive size, gentle nature, and loya temperament.

Key Facts

  • Origin: England
  • Size: Large to giant-sized dogs, typically 23-28 inches tall
  • Weight: 120 - 230 pounds
  • Life Span: 7 - 10 years
  • Color: Fawn, black, red, silver, apricot, brindle

Temperament

Mastiffs are known for their:

  • Affectionate and loving thriving on human interaction
  • High intelligence and trainability
  • Playful and gentle demeanor
  • Loyalty and protective
  • Calm and gentle

Care Requirements

To keep a Mastiff happy and healthy:

  • Daily exercise (Short-walks and playtime)
  • Regular grooming (brush 2-3 times weekly)
  • High-quality diet appropriate for large breeds
  • Early socialization and obedience training
  • Regular veterinary check-ups
